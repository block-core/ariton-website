Freedom to Connect, Power to Own

Want to be part of the future of communities? We are seeking investors to help us build the next generation of community building tools.

Hi, I am Sondre. Some of you know me, many of you probably don't. I'm inviting you to join me on a journey to build a new kind of app.

The digital world today is dominated by a few large companies that control the flow of information. We believe that the future of the internet is decentralized, where users own their own data and identities.

We also believe that the world revolves around communities. People come together and form communities of all shapes and kinds, both online and offline. We want to build a platform that empowers these communities to grow and thrive by utilizing the latest in decentralized technologies and open standards.

Ariton is a company that will develop a superapp for communities, under the same name. Ariton is digital company, a decentralized autonomous organization (DAO), that exists only on the Internet.

We are looking for funding to continue the development of the superapp for the next two years.

We are doing a stock sale to raise between 10 and 30 Bitcoin.

Value proposition

As investor and share owner, you will have voting rights in the general assembly for the shares you own. This will give you insight in the development of the company and the superapp, and the ability to help shape the directions and development.

Ariton will have a various ways of earning money from the superapp, the goal is to be self-sustaining and profitable within a few years. The exact details on how Ariton will earn money will be decided as the app develops and users adopt and use the app. As share owner you will be able to earn dividends from the profits of the company.

Ariton will have a token, ARI, that will be used for transactions within the platform. As share owner, you will be able to purchase ARI tokens at a discount when the token is launched. The exact details on the token sale will be decided as we get closer to the launch of the token. The exact details of the discounts will be decided by you, the share owners, in the general assembly.

Stocks

The company currently has issued 370,000 stocks and is issuing 125,000 new stocks for this funding round. Price per stock is 0.00025 BTC.

For this round of funding, we have set a minimum investment of 0.1 BTC (400 stocks) per investor.

Ownership

Ownership of Ariton can be completely anonymous. Ownership will be connected do your decentralized identifier (DID). When you make the final agreement, you can either provide us with your DID, or we will generate and hold the DID for you until the time you want change it.

Exchange

If you want to exchange your stocks in Ariton, you are free to do so. The existing owners of stocks will have the first right to buy the stocks you want to sell, at their current respective shares. If the existing owners do not want to buy the stocks, you are free to sell the stocks to anyone you want. All sales must be reported to the Ariton company for filing and registration connected to the new owner's decentralized identifier.
